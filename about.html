About Our Team

We are a team of four Front-End Development students who collaborated to create this zoo project. Throughout the process, we combined our skills, ideas, and efforts to build an interactive and enjoyable experience. Our team members are Sevilay Guns, Mousumi Mukherjee, Simge Ateş, and Sally Resch. Each member contributed with dedication and teamwork, helping shape this project from start to finish. We’re glad to present the result of our work and hope you enjoy exploring it.
